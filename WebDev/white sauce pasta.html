White Sauce Pasta Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 200g pasta (penne, fusilli, or spaghetti)
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups milk (preferably full-fat)
  • 1/2 cup grated cheese (Parmesan or cheddar, or a mix)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt, to taste
  • Black pepper, to taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil (optional)
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)
  • Olive oil (optional, for drizzling)

Instructions:

  1. Cook the Pasta: In a large pot, bring salted water to a boil. Add the pasta and cook according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain the pasta and set aside, reserving about 1/2 cup of pasta water.
  2. Make the White Sauce: In a separate pan, melt butter over medium heat. Add minced garlic and saut√© for about 1 minute until fragrant. Add the flour and whisk it in to make a smooth roux. Cook the flour for 1-2 minutes until it becomes lightly golden.
  3. Slowly add the milk while whisking continuously to avoid lumps. Keep stirring until the sauce thickens (about 4-5 minutes). Season the sauce with salt, black pepper, oregano, and basil (if using).
  4. Combine Pasta and Sauce: Once the sauce is thickened, add the cooked pasta into the pan with the sauce. Stir to coat the pasta with the sauce. If the sauce is too thick, add a little reserved pasta water to reach your desired consistency.
  5. Add the grated cheese to the pasta and stir until it melts and the sauce becomes creamy.
  6. Garnish and Serve: Serve the pasta hot, garnished with fresh parsley and an optional drizzle of olive oil. Add extra cheese on top if desired.

Enjoy your creamy and delicious White Sauce Pasta!

You can also add vegetables (like mushrooms, spinach, or bell peppers) or grilled chicken for extra flavor and protein.
