Cheese and Beans Toast Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices of bread (whole wheat or white)
  • 1 cup baked beans (canned or homemade)
  • 1 cup grated cheese (cheddar or mozzarella)
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano (optional)
  • Salt, to taste
  • Black pepper, to taste
  • Fresh parsley or basil (optional, for garnish)
  • Chili flakes (optional, for extra heat)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Toast: Toast the bread slices in a toaster or on a grill until golden brown and crispy. Optionally, butter the toasted bread with a little butter on both sides for extra flavor.
  2. Heat the Beans: In a small pan, heat the baked beans over medium heat until they are hot and bubbling. Stir occasionally. Add salt, black pepper, and oregano (if using) to season the beans to your taste.
  3. Prepare the Cheese: Grate the cheese and set it aside. You can use any cheese of your choice, but cheddar and mozzarella work best for melting.
  4. Assemble the Toast: Spoon a generous amount of the heated baked beans onto each toast slice.
  5. Add the Cheese: Sprinkle the grated cheese evenly over the beans. You can add chili flakes on top if you like a bit of spice.
  6. Grill or Bake: Place the prepared toast under a preheated grill (broiler) for about 3-5 minutes, or until the cheese has melted and is bubbling. Alternatively, you can also bake in the oven at 180°C (350°F) for about 5-7 minutes.
  7. Garnish and Serve: Once the cheese has melted, remove the toast from the grill or oven and garnish with fresh parsley or basil (optional). Serve immediately and enjoy your cheesy, hearty toast!

Enjoy your delicious Cheese and Beans Toast!

This is a quick and filling snack or breakfast that can be customized with extra toppings like sautéed mushrooms or tomatoes.
