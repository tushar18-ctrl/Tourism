Russian Salad Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 medium-sized potatoes (about 300g), peeled and diced
  • 1/2 cup carrots (about 60g), peeled and diced
  • 1/2 cup green beans (about 60g), chopped
  • 1/2 cup peas (about 60g), fresh or frozen
  • 1/2 cup boiled corn kernels (about 80g, optional)
  • 1/4 cup pineapple chunks (optional)
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise (about 120g)
  • 1 tablespoon fresh cream (optional, about 15g)
  • Salt, to taste
  • Black pepper, to taste
  • 1/4 teaspoon sugar (optional)
  • Chopped fresh herbs (optional, for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Boil the diced potatoes, carrots, green beans, and peas separately in water with a pinch of salt until tender but not mushy. Drain and set aside to cool completely.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the boiled vegetables along with the corn kernels and pineapple (if using).
  3. Add the mayonnaise and fresh cream (if desired), then mix gently until all ingredients are well-coated.
  4. Season with salt, black pepper, and sugar (if using). Stir gently to combine.
  5. Refrigerate the salad for at least an hour before serving to let the flavors meld. Garnish with chopped herbs before serving.
